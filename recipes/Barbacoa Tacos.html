Mexican Barbacoa Tacos


Description

Barbacoa is an authentic Mexican dish typically made with beef, goat, or lamb. It is traditionally seasoned with dried chilies and spices and slowly cooked until perfectly tender. From there it’s used as a delicious filling for tacos, burritos, and so forth.!


Ingredients

  • Chuck Roast
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Beef Broth
  • Chipotle Chilis
  • Garlic
  • Cumin, cloves, oregano and bay leaves
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Fresh lime juice
  • Hot Sauce
  • Warm Corn Tortillas
  • Peppercorns
  • Broth
  • Dried Chiles

How To make Barbacoa tacos

How to make the beef
  1. Portion beef: Cut roast into 6 portions while removing any large pieces of fat.
  2. Brown portions: Heat oil in skillet and sear seasoned beef, half at a time, until browned on all sides.
  3. Make chili paste: In a food processor, pulse together chipotle chilies, garlic, and 1/4 cup beef broth until well pureed.
  4. Make barbacoa sauce: Whisk together the remaining beef broth with the chipotle mixture, cumin, oregano, salt, pepper and cloves.
  5. Add beef and mixtures to slow cooker: Pour mixture over beef in slow cooker, add bay leaves.
  6. Cook in Crockpot low and slow: Cover and cook on low heat for 8 – 9 hours.
  7. Shred beef: Remove beef from the slow cooker (leave broth) and shred.
  8. Mix in lime and rest to soak flavors: Stir lime juice into broth in slow cooker then return beef to slow cooker and cook on low or warm 20 – 30 minutes longer.
  9. Serve on tacos and Enjoy!
